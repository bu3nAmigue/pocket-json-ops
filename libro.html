Introduction
Patterns included in this book should work with
the majority of drum machines or digital audio
workstations. Bring your own BPM and swing.
Each grid represents a 16-beat measure:
EXAMPLE PATTERN
BD
1
9
SN
5
13

1

Each row represents an instrument:
BD Bass Drum
SN Snare
LT Low Tom
RS Rimshot
MT Medium Tom
CB Cowbell
HT High Tom
CY Cymbal
CL Hand Clap
OH Open High Hat
SH Shaker
CH Closed High Hat
An empty box is simply a rest.
Accented notes ^ can be emphasized, applying a
parameter change, sample change, or effect.
Chain patterns marked “A” or “B" consecutively to
make longer tracks.
“ " indicates a repeated short set of notes,
sometimes called “ratchet” or “beat repeat”.
